You're on a date but Fishita steals your kiss! Go get your revenge and take down that wretched fish.
Use WASD to move and jump. Press LSHIFT to dash. Dashing makes you temporarily immune to damage, and it deflects
any bullets shot at you (not lasers) in addition to hurting Fishita. Try to use that to knock Fishita down so you can
slay her with your dash!
